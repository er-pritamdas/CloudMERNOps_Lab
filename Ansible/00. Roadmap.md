## 3. **Core Concepts**

* **Inventory**

  * Group variables, host variables

* **Playbooks**
  * Anatomy of a playbook (hosts, tasks, handlers, vars)

  * Variables (host_vars, group_vars, extra_vars, facts)

  * Loops (`with_items`, `loop`)

  * Gathering facts
  * `setup` module
